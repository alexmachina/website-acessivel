Athletics

Athletics has been part of the Paralympic Games program since its first edition in Rome-1960. But it was only in 1984 that Brazil won the first medals in the sport, in New York (USA) and in Stoke Mandeville (England). That year, the country won six gold medals, 12 silver and three bronze non-athletics. In total, the country has already won 109 medals in Paralympic Games, 32 gold squares, 47 silver and 30 bronze medals.
